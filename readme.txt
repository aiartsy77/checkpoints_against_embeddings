# Comparison of checkpoints based on embeddings for female faces

Images correspond to batches, which are combinations of embeddings and checkpoints.

## Settings

Prompt:
detailed portrait of extremely beautiful <embedding>, 20yo, flawless skin, detailed eyes, sultry expression, perfectly lit face
Negative:
BadDream, easynegative, pale skin, painted lips, nude, blushing, upper body, wide shot, hands, arms, lying, sitting, breasts, (black and white image)
Sampling method: Euler a
Sampling steps: 50
CFG Scale: 7.5

## Batches

Images were generated in batches and labeled from A1 to E5. Batches are combinations of grouped checkpoints and embeddings. Embeddings were grouped alphabetically, while checkpoints were grouped based on similarity:

A-E are checkpoint groups
1-5 are embedding groups

Checkpoint groups are:
A: 2D and 3D: images resemble cartoon, manga or images from animated movies
B: 2.5D: images that are in between 2D on the one hand and 3D or realistic images on the other
C&D: 2.9D: Almost realistic
E: P: Photoshop realistic images
